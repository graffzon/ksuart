Life happened to me, 2021-2022

The series of photographs titled "Life happened to me" explores the theme of human identity and transformations in life. In these photographs, the faces of the subjects are concealed by various masks, fabrics, and handcrafted headgear, creating a visual metaphor for the changes and shifts that occur in our lives. The series immerses the viewer in an atmosphere of mystery and contemplation about how we mask ourselves in front of the world and how life shapes our appearances.
