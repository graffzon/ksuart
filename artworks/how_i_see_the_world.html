How I see the world, 2021-2022

Through this series of photographs, the author allows the viewer to see the world from a new, more vivid, and emotional perspective, prompting us to pay attention to the beauty and richness of color in our surroundings. This exploration of color and form enables us to reconsider our notions of how we perceive and interpret the world, potentially inspiring us to find fresh ways of experiencing the reality that surrounds us.
