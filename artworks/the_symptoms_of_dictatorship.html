The symptoms of dictatorship, 2022

This is is a deep exploration of a society engulfed in dictatorship. Each image in the series reflects the visual traces of authoritarian rule and attempts to uncover the roots of this issue. The photos capture an atmosphere of oppression, freedom limitations, and individuality loss, prompting reflection on the origins of dictatorships and the factors that sustain them.
