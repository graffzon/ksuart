Surreal Experimentations, 2023

The series of works represents a unique exploration of visual art, where each photograph invites the viewer to look beyond the ordinary and consider new perspectives in the world of photography. These surrealist experiments resonate with the inner world and convey the complexity of human emotions, fantasies, and inner conflicts, creating astonishing and unique images that invite reflection and offer a new perspective on the surrounding reality.
