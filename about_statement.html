Biography Artist statement

My name is Kseniia Antipina, I am multimedia artist based in Berlin. I have got two degrees - the history of art and graphic design in Moscow. I have an enormous experience in visual design. Nowadays, I create photography using self-made headdresses and costumes. Everyday materials are used to create images - fabrics, paper, wire, headbands, scarves, and more. Particular attention is paid to visual aesthetics and colour-related accents. My photography is dedicated to important social issues. Mostly I represent the relationship complexity, the fear of people, the thoughts and feelings. In my photos faces are intentionally hidden. With this, I move the attention away from faces, and instead, viewers experience the visual image as a whole. Thus, viewers are first of all confronted with their own feelings that these artworks invoke in them.
